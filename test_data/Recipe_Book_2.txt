Beef Stew
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup red wine
4 cups beef broth
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 bay leaves
4 carrots, peeled and sliced
3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then brown in batches, adding more oil as needed. Remove beef and set aside.
Reduce heat to medium. Add onions and garlic, cooking until onions are translucent.
Sprinkle flour over the onions and stir to coat. Cook for 2 minutes.
Pour in wine and scrape the bottom of the pot to loosen any browned bits.
Add beef broth, tomato paste, thyme, and bay leaves. Return beef to the pot.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 1.5 hours.
Add carrots and potatoes to the pot. Cook for another 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
Remove bay leaves and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper before serving.

Chicken Salad
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup red grapes, halved
1/4 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
Lettuce leaves, for serving
Instructions:
In a large bowl, combine chicken, celery, grapes, and almonds.
In a separate small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard until smooth.
Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and toss to coat evenly.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Chill in the refrigerator for at least one hour to blend the flavors.
Serve on a bed of lettuce leaves.

Fish Tacos
Ingredients:
1 pound white fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
8 corn tortillas
1/2 cabbage, shredded
1 avocado, sliced
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
Lime wedges, for serving
Salsa or hot sauce, for serving
Instructions:
Season fish fillets with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add fish and cook for 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through and flaky.
Warm tortillas in a dry skillet over low heat.
Break the fish into chunks and divide among the tortillas.
Top each taco with shredded cabbage, avocado slices, and chopped cilantro.
Serve with lime wedges and salsa or hot sauce.
